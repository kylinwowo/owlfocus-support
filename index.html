FAQ

  • How to blur inactive windows?
    OwlFocus automatically blurs all inactive windows. Just focus on the active one.
  • Does OwlFocus require system permissions?
    No. OwlFocus works without system authorization, keeping your data safe.
  • Can I control OwlFocus with OpenClaw?
    Yes. OwlFocus supports direct OpenClaw commands for automation.
